Message from Kamran Lashari

It has been a pleasure and a privilege to serve Gymkhana and its esteemed members for the last three years period. When we commenced this journey, the club had fallen victim to stagnation, deterioration and unfortunate aesthetic environment that was outdated and not commensurate with its image or history. I had promised to initiate our journey with the restoration of the club to re-gain its aura and glory. And Alhamdolillah, we have pursued the same with the dedication. Relentlessly, we pursued excellence and sought loftier targets and higher benchmarks! I can state with humility that the ornamentation and transformation is an undeniable fulfilment of our campaign promises! We pledged to challenge the status quo and stagnation and arrest sluggish growth, interest and deterioration and with Allah’s Grace and your support and appreciation the transformation is evident! manifest! and continuing! Gymkhana has regained it’s prestige, image and standing!

We remained focused on enhancing the existing members’ membership value, experience, increased frequenting, greater subscriptions, and increased services utilisation! Gymkhana now has an unparalleled, estimable and enviable ambience and service.

The metamorphosis is not just in aesthetics and visual. Sustainability, long-term viability and members’ experience are all fundamental fiduciary duties held sacred by us. We exhibited financial excellence and prudence in adding to the clubs assets - in particular revenue generating assets; while continuing to maintain strong liquidity; and enhancing and expanding revenue streams.

Let us celebrate and keep the journey going against a resilient contextual backdrop of stronger revenues and expanding revenue streams; capital investments into successful revenue generating assets; and strong liquidity in a short value growth duration. Let us celebrate the fact that we have laid an inviolable foundation for long-term sustainability and continued growth! We can continue this or we can let irresponsible petty leadership destroy this!

We can all indulge in rhetoric, negativity, destructive snd politically inspired negative criticism!!!! Or we can all celebrate the renewed pride we can take as members in the accomplishments of this illustrious club. We can celebrate the improved aesthetics; investment into facilities and expansions; uplift in sporting and gaming facilities; enriched and expanded culinary experience; diverse and rich entertainment activities; and events and quite simply the overall enriched enhanced and enjoyable membership experience.

Those who criticise, and create a petty hullabaloo, do so because they know they cannot talk about real issues! Because they cannot deny the incredible performance during times of economic distress! They cannot negate the significantly increased frequency of member visits to the club for food and beverage, sports and other activities. They can see that subscription and club usage have increased with no increase in subscription rates!! Covers in eating facilities have increased year on year! They cannot counted with anything the momentous enhancement in members’ comfort and convenience and ease of facility usage!

But I don’t have to say much the club speaks for itself: this is a transformed club with unrivalled beauty – an oasis of tranquillity in the hum drum of the city’s heart - a second home to all of us; a place of enriched social experience where you experience intellect, entertainment and sports! I don’t have to say much: your patronage and support speaks for itself!

But we need to speak louder because the opposition can’t hear! They want to take the club back to a stagnant stalemate situation! Do you want to expose the club to leadership crisis where no clear leadership, vision, values and manifesto is articulated!

Let us not stop the path of performance and progress! Let us continue the journey of pursuit of excellence and sustainability.

Kamran Lashari

OUR Mission

The provision of a high quality social experience for our members, pursuing excellence sustainably.

This mission will be achieved by having:

  • An efficiently-managed club that has sound financial foundations.
  • A golf course, tennis court, swimming facility, gym and other sporting facilities that are challenging and yet enjoyable for golfers, athletes and sportspersons of all abilities.
  • High-quality and comfortable clubhouse with first-class hospitality and a variety of social events.

OUR Values

  • Responsive to Members needs: “Members first in everything we do”
  • Welcoming, friendly, and inclusive yet maintaining standards of dignity commensurate with the club’s image.
  • Open, transparent and accountable decision-making.
  • Honesty, integrity and fair dealings with members, visitors and all stakeholders.
  • Commitment to a safe and healthy environment.
  • Friendly service from a well-trained and talented team of staff.
